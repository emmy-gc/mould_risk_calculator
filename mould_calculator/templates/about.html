About the Mould Risk Calculator

Understanding indoor air quality for healthier living environments

The Mould Risk Calculator is an interactive tool designed to assess the likelihood of mould development based on environmental conditions such as temperature and humidity. It processes time-series datasets and uses scientifically grounded models to evaluate potential mould growth over time.

Good air quality is essential for a healthy home. Our mould risk calculator helps you understand when your indoor environment might be at risk. Simple to use and built for everyone.
